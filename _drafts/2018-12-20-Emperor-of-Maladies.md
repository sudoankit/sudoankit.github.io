### Prologue

You often get those scares in life that makes you depressed, stressed and subsequently sad. Many take the easy route to end them, narcotics, tobacco or an even easier route, suicide.

You don't have to. You don't need them. It's of no use. This post isn't about them. I'm not writing to give you advice, to stop and improve. You already know it. 

You fucking know it very well.

This post isn't also about cancer, why tobacco causes cancer, genes, chemotherapy or radiation, painkillers or surgery.

It isn't about holding back tears, about kissing young ones goodnight for the last time, being afraid and give up.

"What is it then?"

### Seed

The "seed" is the foundation a human's mind is made with. The sapling, then grows when the human dies. Over the years, it becomes a beautiful tree that's etched in memory. We remember the good that was in the human. 

Don't you?

I believe life is about making that tree the most beautiful. The more people remember you, the more flowers bloom, branches divide and the tree grows. 

You need to start first, at the "seed".

### Story time.

My uncle was a school teacher. A damn good one. Sadly, he was a victim of chains of wrong decisions that led him to be a school teacher at a remote village in India with minimal pay. A hope for a better future made him continue teaching and home tutoring many, many young children for extra pay and (nuturing that seed.) 

He married. Still the same job though. 

More students got tutioned and good karma nurtured that seed even more.

He became father. A beautiful boy, oh the joy!
