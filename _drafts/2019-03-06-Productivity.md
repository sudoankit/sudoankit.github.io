# Being Yourself

I write about my experience with stoicism, mediations and forming good habits.

> Meditation helps strengthen your stoicism which in turn allows you form great habits and maintain them.

I used to be extremely carefree -- void of any great habits and the ones I had usually lasted less than a month.

Sleeping late, no exercise, surfing the web mindlessly and wasting time on unproductive activities such as binge watching movies and TV, playing video games and *stuff I am ashamed to write here.* 

My conscience always reminded me to stop it but my body never had the discipline to listen and act. 

Controlling your body and mind is the key to success. 

***Discipline.***
